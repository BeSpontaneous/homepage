About Me

I am a first-year PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department, Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Yun Raymond Fu. Prior to this, I obtained my bachelor degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. My research interests include Computer Vision and Deep Learning, especially Dynamic Networks and Model Efficiency.
